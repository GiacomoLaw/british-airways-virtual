Events leader

We currently require an event leader. It involves leading the event we organise. Please check below for the requirements.

  • Must have Infinite Flight Live, valid for three or more months at the time of applying.
  • Must be grade 3 or above on IF live.
  • Must have over 100 hours of flying time.
  • Events by BA virtual often occur at 1100z on Saturdays and Sundays, so the applicant must be free at that time on those days.
  • On the Infinite Flight Community, preferably for over 100 days and a regular.

    • If you are interested in this job, please email me at the email given above with proof of the above things. I will get back to you quickly, and thank you for applying!

    Flying time logger

    This job involves the applicant logging time that people have flown. People submit their flight time in minutes on a channel in the VA Slack, and the flight time logger is responsible for helping adding the time logged to the spreadsheet.

  • Must be a trusted member on the Infinite Flight Community.
  • Must be competent with spreadsheets.
  • A Google account is required, as Google Sheets is used to log time.

    • Intrested? Make sure to send me an email. If you would like more information, you can also request that in an email.
